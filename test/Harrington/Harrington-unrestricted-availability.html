Harrington HealthCare System is currently offering vaccination for all those who qualify for vaccination under Massachusetts Covid-19 Vaccine Distribution Timeline Please visit https://www.mass.gov/covid-19-vaccine prior to registering for an appointment to determine if you qualify - no exceptions are made



At your appointment, you may be asked for the following information:

Insurance card. Vaccination is free whether you have insurance or not, if you have insurance, please bring that information with you.

Identification, examples include:

  • Employer-issued ID card that includes your name and title; or
  • Government-issues identification or license; or
  • Recent pay stub

You may get a vaccination even if you do not have a driver’s license or social security number


***This form is for initial immunization sign-up only. If you have already received the initial vaccine you would have been given your return appointment. An appointment reminder for your 2nd vaccine dose will be sent via call/text message to your phone number on file.***

***For those who have a history of anaphylactic reactions, please contact the vaccination center at 508-765-8160, as your visit may need to be vaccinated in a hospital setting.

***Vaccination is only offered to those 18 years of age and older
