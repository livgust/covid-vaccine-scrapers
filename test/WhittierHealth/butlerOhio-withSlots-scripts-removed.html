BCGHD COVID-19 Response

BCGHD Vaccination Clinic April 2nd: 18 years of age and older

This event is scheduled for April 2nd, 2021.

Location of the event: 1715 Fairgrove Ave, Hamilton OH 45011

FIRST DOSE ONLY - DRIVE THROUGH EVENT

This event registration is for Ohioans 18 years of age and older living or working in Butler County ONLY.

Vaccine Available: Moderna

Vaccine recipients must be age 18 or older to be eligible for the Moderna vaccine. 18 is the lowest age authorized by the FDA for use of the Moderna vaccine.

Both doses of the series should be completed with the same product. Pfizer and Moderna vaccines are not interchangeable with each other or with other covid-19 vaccine products (your second dose should be from the same manufacturer as your first)

If you have had any injectable vaccine or immunotherapy/blood products in the last 14 days, you are not eligible to receive vaccine.

YOU NEED TO PRINT AND FILL OUT THE REGISTRATION PAPERWORK IN ADVANCE, AND BRING WITH YOU TO THE CLINIC, WHICH CAN BE FOUND AT THE END OF THIS MESSAGE.

*If you are unable to print, you can complete the forms on-site and may cause minor delays in the vaccination process.

You must register individuals one at a time. Once you complete one registration you can go back into the sign-up and register another person.

You will receive a confirmation email with instructions for the day of the event as well as the required registrations forms. If you do not receive this confirmation email your appointment may NOT have been scheduled correctly.

You will be prompted to hit the "SUBMIT" button TWICE during this process (1) after selecting a preferred time and (2) after inputing your contact information/answering screening questions

Please arrive at your scheduled time, but not before. This will allow us to move traffic smoothly and efficiently. If you arrive too early this will cause us traffic jam and you may be turned away until your scheduled arrival time.

YOUR SECOND SHOT WILL BE SCHEDULED ON APRIL 30th. YOU WILL BE CONTACTED SOON TO SCHEDULE A TIME SLOT.

THIS EVENT IS SUBJECT TO CANCELLATION IF VACCINE BECOMES UNAVAILABLE.
