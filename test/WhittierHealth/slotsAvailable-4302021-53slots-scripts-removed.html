BRN Public First Dose

4/30 WRH Bradford COVID First Dose Clinic

This clinic is for those receiving their first dose of the Moderna vaccine.  Once you receive the first dose, you will be automatically scheduled for the follow-up dose. 

This will be for anyone 18 years of age or older who lives, works or studies in Massachusetts.  Please note that although the state guideline allows for vaccination of anyone 16 or older, the FDA has authorized use of the Moderna vaccine only to individuals 18 and older.  

NOTE:  if you do not meet either of these above criteria and are simply accompanying someone who does, you are not eligbile to receive the vaccine here at Whittier under the Governor's guidance as that only applies to the "mass" vaccination sites (Fenway, Gillette, Eastfield Mall and Doubletree Danvers).  

Please review the available slots below and click on the button to sign up. 

It is imperative that you print, fill out, and bring attached consent form with you to speed up the registration process. 

Thank you!
